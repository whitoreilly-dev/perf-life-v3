Who It's For

You don't have to figure this out alone.

Perf Life coaching is built for three distinct groups — each with a tailored approach.

Primary Focus

Single Parents & Solo Decision-Makers

Carrying every decision without a partner to think it through with. Perf Life provides trusted, grounded, non-judgmental partnership — focused on positive momentum forward.

Also Served

Adults in Transition

Rebuilding after divorce, grief, burnout, job loss, or a major life shift. You need structure, steadiness, and real traction — not platitudes.

Also Served

Young Adults Building Direction

Overwhelmed by choices and wanting a clear framework. Early-career individuals building confidence and a repeatable decision-making approach.

Session Formats

Choose how you work best.

1:1 Life Navigation Coaching

Core Offering

Personalized sessions to help you think clearly and move with intention. Bring whatever is most alive — we'll work through it together with structure and heart. Sessions are available weekly, bi-weekly, or as-needed.

"Be Your Own Leader" Program

Program

A structured engagement using the Personal Organizational Chart and Compass Method to build the self-leadership tools you'll use for life. Ideal for clients who want a framework, not just a sounding board.

Weekly Accountability Sessions

Ongoing

A consistent checkpoint to maintain positive momentum forward — week after week. Shorter, focused, and designed to keep you in motion between longer coaching sessions.

Walk & Talk Coaching

Zoom · Movement-Based

Think better while moving. Both Merrily and the client walk during the session — separately, over Zoom — for reflection with momentum. Particularly effective for clients who process better when not sitting still.

The Process

How it works.

Simple, unhurried, and designed to fit into your actual life.

1

Discovery Session

Complimentary. 45–60 minutes. Bring what's most on your mind. We sort it together and identify the clearest next step.

2

First Deep-Dive Session

A focused look at your current landscape using the Personal Org Chart and Compass Method to build your foundation tools.

3

Ongoing Partnership

Regular sessions at a cadence that fits your life. Progress tracked. Tools refined. Momentum maintained.
