Perf Pod Club

Listen. Reflect. Discuss. Act.

The Perf Pod Club is a membership community centered on curated listening — podcast episodes, conversations, and discussions tied directly to the themes of Perf Life coaching.

Each week, members get a prompt, a listen, and a place to reflect with others navigating similar seasons of life. It's coaching adjacent — not a replacement for 1:1 work, but a meaningful layer of ongoing support and community.

  • ✓ Weekly curated listening prompt
  • ✓ Private community discussion
  • ✓ Monthly live group session with Merrily
  • ✓ Worksheets and reflection tools
