The Approach

Structure with a heartbeat.

Two core anchors keep your decision-making clear, no matter what season of life you're in.

Framework 1

The Compass Method

Define your true values and priorities. Use them as your decision compass. When choices feel impossible, your Compass cuts through the noise.

Framework 2

Personal Org Chart — Be Your Own CEO

Map your Foundation People, Challengers, responsibilities, and outside Influencers so you can lead your life with intention — not reaction.

Framework 3

Rebirth Map

Daily, weekly, and monthly mapping of where you are, where you want to be, and what you're ready to keep or release.

Framework 4

Board of Directors

Identify who gives you meaningful feedback and objective truth — even when it's hard to hear.
