Founder

Merrily Mellick, M.A.

Merrily Mellick is the founder of Perf Life — Coaching & Life Navigation Consulting. She holds a Master's in Organizational Psychology from Columbia University's Teachers College and brings over two decades of experience in leadership development, human resources, and personal growth consulting.

Her background spans serving as Director of Human Resources at Juno Online Services during the early internet era, to Business Development Consultant for True Colors International. She combines professional expertise with deep empathy and lived experience to guide individuals through life's challenges and transformations.

Her approach — blending realism, humor, and heart — helps clients cultivate clarity, self-leadership, and authentic fulfillment.

The Approach

Structure that still feels human.

Six proprietary tools and frameworks designed to help you lead your own life with intention.

Personal Organizational Chart — Be Your Own CEO

Map your Foundation People, Challengers, responsibilities, and outside Influencers so you can lead your life with intention — not reaction.

The Compass Method

Define your true values and priorities, then use them as your decision compass. Values can evolve, but you always know your direction.

Rebirth Map

Daily, weekly, and monthly mapping of where you are, where you want to be, and what you are ready to keep or release.

Board of Directors

Identify who gives you meaningful feedback and objective truth — even when it is hard to hear.

Shoulds Inventory

Separate inherited expectations from what is authentically yours. Quiet the noise of other people's timelines.

Walk & Talk Sessions

Zoom coaching while both parties walk — for movement-based reflection and clearer thinking. Because sometimes the body knows first.

Important to Know

Coaching, not therapy.

This distinction matters — and Merrily takes it seriously.

What coaching is

Forward-focused partnership. Clarity tools. Decision structure. Accountability. A non-judgmental thinking partner for the complexity of your everyday life.

What coaching is not

Coaching is not a substitute for licensed therapy, medical care, or legal and financial advice. Merrily will always refer out when the right professional is someone else.

Referral Network

The right expert, every time.

When clients need expertise beyond coaching, they are connected with trusted professionals including licensed therapists, psychiatrists, physicians, attorneys, financial planners, career coaches, and specialized grief resources.

This isn't a hand-off — it's a partnership. Merrily continues to be your thinking partner while the right specialist supports you in their domain.
